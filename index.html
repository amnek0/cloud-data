Methodology

Notes on quality of data

Retrieve top 250 sites in the US from Alexa. For each name get its IP address. Then, find the organization that owns the IP address through ARIN's API.

Known issues:

  • Addresses not managed by ARIN are not mapped.
  • Some IP addresses don't have orgs publicly listed.
  • If a zone is managed by third party (Akamai or CloudFlare might be common), they might be acting as proxy.
  • Many places use round-robin DNS (different IPs managed by different orgs).
